About us

Elvis Sibeko Studios was first conceptualized in 2014 by Elvis Sibeko an international acclaim award winning dancer and choreographer.

Through his experience in theatre and entertainment for more than 10 years, a powerhouse for multimedia entertainment productions was established. ES Studios serves to output authentic and impactful African inspired content, directly from the grassroots of Africa to the global world.

Vision

An Innovative and competent umbrella of Afrocentric entertainment

Mission

To be a world leading transformative Afrocentric multi-enterprise for entertainment and information

Our Objectives

  • To affirm Afrocentric quality entertainment
  • To produce high quality and epic family experience with afro futuristic approach
  • To promote a new innovative and transformative Afrocentric media enterprise
