The heart and passion of giving back

Coined by Elvis Sibeko to ignite the spirit of ubuntu in philanthropy. Ubuntu + Philanthropy = Ubunthropy means helping each other regardless and beyond personal gain, but through the love and passion of helping and making a difference without expectation.

In the spirit of Ubunthropy we can build a greater world of mankind, to live in peace and harmony united as one.

Connecting the world through local community development

In ES Studios we work to improve our community through the creation of Arts and Culture development for opportunities.

We believe that to reach a world of an ultimate edition, we need to work on the local boundaries off the current. We help disadvantage and marginalised communities and give them opportunities and implement transformative programmes that build a better future.
