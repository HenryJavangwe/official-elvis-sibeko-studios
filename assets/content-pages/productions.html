Visualizing entertainment for a greater purpose

ES Production is a heart of producing high African aesthetic productions on a broad and diverse international entertainment level.
We focus on qualities of African music, theatre, dance, film, poetry and novels productions. The trans-genre approach aims to create entertainment of high quality, with new and innovative representation of African content.

In our productions we aim to visualize entertainment for a greater purpose. We develop content for African entertainment and for conceptualizing narratives that inspires the world for change.

Our inspiration is to work with local and international artists with a multicultural and global perspective. We create artistic productions with the attention to the interest of children and adults

We produce theatre performances, live art and public arts performances, arts and culture festivals, community and school lecture demonstration of Arts and Culture that are transformative and promotes social change and inclusion.
